Like many, I love breakfast.

BreakFirst 101’s is still about impeccable attention to detail in our food and service, but in a more relaxed and casual family dining atmosphere.

BreakFirst 101’s serves an all-day breakfast and Bistro menu designed to rouse the senses. Freshly baked bread and homemade jams, curds, and yogurt as well as prepared meats—all these add to the restaurant’s rustic charm.

French Toast

French toast is a dish made of sliced bread soaked in beaten eggs and typically milk, then pan fried. In France, French toast is actually called “pain perdu”, which means “lost bread”. Back in a time where food was sparse, and certainly nothing wasted, this dish was a way to use up all the bread from the days before that had gone stale. source
